Pig Game

JavaScript, CSS, HTML

https://oliver-leonor.github.io/pig-game/

The Pig game is a simple dice game where players take turns rolling a die. The goal is to reach a certain score, such as 100 points, before the other player. On each turn, a player can roll the die as many times as they want, but if they roll a 1, they lose all their points for that turn. The player can also choose to hold their points and end their turn at any time. The first player to reach the target score wins..
